The 4-1-1 on the Lingo

Doctrine

Think of it as a country's main belief or rule for how it acts. Like a "no drama" policy for your friend group.

Foreign Policy

This is the country's game plan for dealing with other nations. Who are its allies? Who are its rivals?

Nationalism

This is next-level team pride for your country. Believing your nation is the best and being super proud of it.
